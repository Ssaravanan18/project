SARAVANAN S

The name Saravanan, a male Tamil name, is special as it means "thickets of reeds" and is also a name for the Hindu god Murugan, often associated with the reed-filled lake in the Tamil script. It's a traditional name, often given to boys and can also be used as a patronymic surname in Tamil culture. The name is also connected to the popular Tamil actor Suriya Sivakumar, whose real name is Saravanan.
